How It Works

1. Upload Your Images

Select and upload your sketch and target images. Ensure both images are in a suitable format and resolution.

2. Image Processing

Our CGAN model processes the images to generate the output. This involves a generator and discriminator working together to transform your sketch into a detailed face.

3. Compare Results

The generated image is compared with the target image using L2 and SSIM scores. This helps assess the quality and accuracy of the generated face.
